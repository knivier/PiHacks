About PiHacks

PiHacks 2026 is an online hackathon for Pioneer High School students only. The competition runs from April 1st through May 1st. Whether you have years of experience or have never written a line of code, you belong here—build at your own pace, learn as you go, and compete for cash prizes. Judging is online.

Register to secure your spot and help us plan. Teams can have up to four people; larger teams (3–4) are scored on a harsher scale.

Competition Tracks

We leave you to choose your own adventure. However, we have some tracks we recommend you consider.

🤖

AI / ML

Models, assistants, computer vision—if it learns or predicts, it fits here.

🎮

Gaming

Games of any kind: web, desktop, mobile, or experimental—make something fun to play.

🤝

Social Good

Projects that help your school, community, or a cause you care about.

🛠️

Developer Tool

CLIs, libraries, productivity apps, or anything that makes other developers’ lives easier.

Important Information

🎓 Who can join

Open to Pioneer High School students only. Beginners and experienced coders are all welcome.

👥 Teams

Up to four people per team. Projects from 3–4 person teams are weighted on a harsher scale than smaller teams.

🌐 Format & judging

This is an online hackathon; judging is online. The April 10 check-in is the required in-person milestone for participants.

📋 Participation

Public voting (May 5–10) is required for prize eligibility. Final submission deadline: May 1 at 8:00 PM EST, no exceptions.
